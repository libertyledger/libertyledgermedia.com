Submitting Tips Safely

Liberty Ledger Media takes source protection seriously. If you have sensitive information:

  • Use a non-work email or phone when contacting us
  • Do not use your employer's network or devices
  • Consider using encrypted communication (we can provide guidance)
  • We never reveal sources without explicit permission
  • We will work with you to protect your identity

Anonymous tip system coming soon. We're building encrypted submission tools to ensure maximum source protection.

What We Investigate

We're interested in tips about:

  • Government contracts (especially no-bid or questionable awards)
  • Conflicts of interest among officials
  • Wasteful spending or budget irregularities
  • Civil rights violations
  • Law enforcement misconduct
  • Campaign finance violations
  • Public records being withheld improperly
  • Corruption, fraud, or abuse of power

Every credible tip is investigated. We don't drop stories.
